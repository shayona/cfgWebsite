In Partnership With Blue Dragons

Founded in 2003, Blue Dragon Children’s Foundation is a non-profit organisation based in Vietnam whose aim is to help street kids and children who have been affected by slavery and human trafficking. Blue Dragon’s Safe and Sound program works with Vietnamese and Chinese authorities to rescue women and children who have been trafficked, most of them into China, helping them to return home and educating whole communities on how to avoid further trafficking incidents. They are working with the local authorities to better understand the nature of human trafficking in Vietnam and how to respond to such situations, whilst offering long-term counselling and school support to those affected by trafficking.

Project X

Project X is Blue Dragon’s program to provide psychosocial support for survivors of sex trafficking, as the rescue is only the first part of a long and difficult journey of recovery for girls and women who have been trafficked. Blue Dragon has established emergency shelters for girls and women who have just escaped sex trafficking, as well as a network of ongoing assistance to return to school, university or other training to ensure a brighter future for these survivors. As part of the Project X program, the girls and women have regular medical checkups, and care and nutrition to get them back to good physical health, as well as receiving psychological counselling to recover from both short-term and long-term trauma.

There are many potential uses for this type of app, but the main focuses would most likely be to support Blue Dragon, to inform of threats, and to create a support system, or network. The main ways in which this app would support Blue Dragon’s efforts would be to have feature that victims could make use of to inform blue dragon as well as to help Blue Dragon to better and more effectively inform vulnerable populations/individuals. The support system/network could be used to let victims get in touch with each other thus creating a type of support structure. This could also be a tool for those willing to share their story to be able to do so, which could also help inform vulnerable individuals and create a safer of environment for those who have experienced similar trauma. This network can also be used in order for information sharing to occur between Blue Dragon and communities, as well as between communities.
