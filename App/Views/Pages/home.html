Our Goal

Coding For Good aims to help others through the use of digital code.

Whether you need a website for your GC or an app for your service, Coding For Good will help you every step of the way.

Started in 2017 by UWCSEA East High School Physics teacher David Kann and a small handful of students, Coding for Good has evolved into a group of more than 15 students, still with the same goal in mind.
